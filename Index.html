Promoting Mental Health Awareness for All Filipinos

🧠 What is Mental Health?

Mental health affects how we think, feel, and act. In the Philippines, many still hide their struggles due to stigma. Let’s change that—together.

⚠️ Common Mental Health Issues

  • Depression: Affects millions of Filipinos
  • Anxiety: Especially among students and workers
  • Suicide: Leading cause of death in young people
  • Stigma: Prevents people from seeking help

📞 Where to Get Help

  • NCMH Hotline: 1553
  • Hopeline PH: 0917-558-4673
  • Mind You PH: mindyou.com.ph
